Bank

MONUMENT AND BANK STATIONS: Please avoid using Monument Station to interchange with services from Bank. Until spring 2009 interchange between District and Circle line services at Monument and all services from Bank station is only available at street level due to escalator refurbishment works. As an alternative use nearby Mansion House or Cannon Street stations which are a short walk from Bank. Maps of the area are available at both stations. Local bus services 21 43 133 and 141 also operate between London Bridge and Moorgate stations calling at Bank and Monument.

Interchange at the station may involve walking routes at street level.  Oyster Pay-As-You-Go users need to interchange within 30 minutes to avoid being charged twice.
